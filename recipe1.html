Classic Sourdough Bread Recipe

This is my signature recipe for classic sourdough bread!

Ingredients

  • 500g bread flour
  • 300g water
  • 100g sourdough starter
  • 10g salt

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, water, and sourdough starter until combined. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Add salt and knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  3. Let the dough rise for 4-6 hours at room temperature.
  4. Shape the dough and let it proof for another 2 hours.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 230°C (450°F) for 30-35 minutes.
